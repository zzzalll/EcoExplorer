About Us

EcoExplorer is an innovative collaboration between Fakulti Alam Bina (FAB) and Fakulti Sains dan Matematik (FSKM), designed to highlight the groundbreaking research conducted by FAB students in a dynamic and engaging format. This initiative serves as a platform for students to present their work through interactive exhibits, immersive experiences, and digital storytelling, making complex sustainability concepts accessible to a wider audience. By blending scientific discovery with creative engagement, EcoExplorer fosters a deeper appreciation for environmental stewardship and sustainable development. Through workshops, hands-on activities, and thought-provoking discussions, the project encourages visitors to actively participate in sustainability efforts while gaining insights into the latest advancements in eco-friendly solutions. Ultimately, EcoExplorer aims to inspire positive change, promote interdisciplinary collaboration, and strengthen the connection between academic research and real-world environmental impact.
